About Me

"The devil is in the details, but so is salvation." - Hymen G. Rickover

This is a quote that has stuck with me throughout my entire career.

From when I first learned it as a junior sailor in the Navy, it has been central to my development and approach to projects throughout the near-15 years of Naval service, in my current role as a Power Plant Engineer, and into my dream job of a Full Stack Developer. One common theme that resonates with me is that being a detail orientated person, I developed a unique ability to be analytical in my problem solving. This synergistic blend works great on my own, but where I truly succeed is collaborating with a team. I decided to pursue this career path because I have always been interested in tech and how it can be used to make our lives more efficient and allow for time to do the things we love.

My goal-orientated and growth mindset when it comes to a team has, on a number of occasions, helped the team I was a part of on task and deliver our product either on time or even early. Due to the skills and various roles I have performed as I developed in the Navy and after enables me to look at a problem from different perspectives and be able to provide a complete solution.

I'm now looking forward to honing my Front End Development skills with a company that values a growth mindset and developing their staff.
